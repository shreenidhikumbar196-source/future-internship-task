df.dropna(inplace=True)

df['Purchase_Date'] = pd.to_datetime(df['Purchase_Date'])

# Create Month (as string for better plotting)
df['Month'] = df['Purchase_Date'].dt.strftime('%Y-%m')
In [4]:
df['Sales'] = df['Price'] * df['Quantity']

Key Insights¶

  • Customers in the 26–35 age group contribute the highest share of total sales, indicating strong purchasing power in this segment.

  • A significant portion of revenue is driven by a specific customer segment (age 26–35, male), highlighting a concentrated customer base.

FINAL INSIGHTS¶

  • A significant portion of leads are not converted, indicating drop-offs in the sales funnel
  • Certain lead types perform better, showing higher conversion rates
  • Some business segments have stronger conversion performance than others
  • Converted customers tend to have higher average monthly revenue, indicating better quality leads
  • There is a clear opportunity to optimize underperforming segments and improve overall conversion rate
